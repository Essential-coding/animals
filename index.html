    Do animal matter?

    They play a cruical role!

    Animals are important for our environment. We are dependent of the animals and their materials.

    We use them for food and for traveling as well as for fur and other material things. We even use some of them for hunting food and for protection.

    They are also a part of the food chain which is crucial for the biological world that we live in.

    Does the food chain matter?

    Its important for our survival

    Apex predator

    Apex predators are the highest in the food chain and the golden eagles are one of them. That means that they are the top consumers of the food chain without natural predators.

    Another word for Apex predators is called Tertiary consumers however when it comes to Apex predators , they are the highest one in the food chain meaning they can also eat other tertiary consumers even if they are the tertiary.

    For the eagle they are carnivores meaning they do not eat vegetables but only meat!

    Tertiary consumer

    Snakes are Tertiary consumers that means that they recieve their nutrition by eating meat.

    Snakes are not vegeterian, therefore they are called carnivores!

    Secondary cosumer / Tertiary consumer

    Frog are actually Tertiary consumers but in this food chain they are placed as secondary consumer because the snake wins the tertiary place here.

    However frogs eat meat and can eat up too a hundred insects a day! That is why a frog is called tertiary consumer since they eat meat.

    They feed on grasshoppers and other types of pests. They usually live near the waters.

    Primary Consumer

    The grasshopper is one of the primary consumers that is called a herbivore. That means that they eat plants to survive.

    However it can have a omnivorous behavior meaning that they can eat on animal tissues and wastes. Their habitat is the grasslands and tropical forests.

    The Producers

    Grass are one of the key producers in the food chain. Producers is organism that is self-sustained.

    It gets its energy from the sun and therefore provide energy such as organic substances that it puts into the food chain therefore it plays the most important roles for the food-chain.

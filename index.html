    About The Animals

    I will talk about several types of different animals across the globe that we live in. I will also describe what the animals do and what type of animals it is.

    Different animals

    Camel

    A camel is an even-toed ungulate in the genus Camelus that bears distinctive fatty deposits known as "humps" on its back.

    Camels have long been domesticated and, as livestock, they provide food and textiles.

    Snow Leopard

    The Snow Leopards powerful build allows it to travel quickly.

    Its tail provide great balance and agility and also protects it from the cold.

    The Snow Leopard is found in twelve countries, stretching from central asia to south asia.

    Read More
    Lion

    Lions live in a variety of habitats but usually live in environnments like in the grassland, savanna and open woodlands.

    The African lions are the most social cats of all the cats around the globe.

    Read More
    Golden Eagle

    Golden Eagles are considered one of the best fliers amongst all birds.

    It can travel with a speed of 320km/h and they use their sharp talons to hunt for rabbits and other animals like fish.

    Does animal matter?

    They play a crucial role!

    Animals are important for our environment. We are dependent of the animals and their materials.

    We use them for food and for traveling as well as for fur and other material things. We even use some of them for hunting food and for protection.

    They are also a part of the food chain which is crucial for the biological world that we live in.

    The Food Chain

    The Apex Predator

    The Apex Predator are the animals that are in the top of the food chain.

    They have no natural enemies. In this case the eagles are considered carnivores and the reason are simple, they only feed on meat.

    Tertiary consumer

    Snakes are Tertiary consumers that means that they recieve their nutrition by eating meat.

    Snakes are not vegeterian, therefore they are called carnivores!
